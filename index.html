About Us

Intelligent Information Processing Group (Laboratory of Intelligent Systems and Applications, LISA) is a leading research group at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Hunan University of Science and Technology.


Our research focuses on cutting-edge technologies in intelligent information processing, with particular emphasis on side-channel attack and defense and intelligent mining equipment development. We address fundamental theoretical challenges while developing practical solutions for real-world applications.


Security Focus

Side-channel analysis and hardware security research
